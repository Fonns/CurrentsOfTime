The Prophecy Will Be Fulfilled

When Cronos learned of the prophecy that one of his children would supplant him as ruler of the world, he attemped to imprision the Gods of Olympus in Tartarus, a dungeon of torment and suffering. However, Zeus avoided that fate and went into exile.

You play as Kairos, a Greek mercenary who has secretly been given the Scythe of Time by a mysterious woman. Your adventure begins with the quest to find Zeus - the only one who will have the power to permanently defeat Cronos and fullfill the prophecy.
